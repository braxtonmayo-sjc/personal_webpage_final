Welcome to my Website

Hi! My name is Braxton Mayo. I'm 20 years old and I'm a student at San Jac working toward a Fine Arts AA.
I have always had an interest in art and hope to eventually work in 2D animation -- either on a full-length feature film or an animated series.
I enjoy exploring different art mediums and learning new skills and techniques. My current favorites are digital art and photography.
In addition to being a full time student, I work part time at my grandfather's machine shop and have an ebay store re-selling merch from popular animation series.
I have had the opportunity to photograph several fundraising events for Autism Speaks Houston and hope to expand into doing more freelance photography work in the future.
You can check out some of my work below - enjoy!
