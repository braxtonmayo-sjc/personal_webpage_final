Welcome to my Website

Hi! My name is Braxton Mayo. I'm 20 years old. This is my 3rd year at San Jac because I just switched to an Art and Design AAS last semester after taking 2 years of basics for a General Studies AA. I am interested in animation, digital art, and photography. I am taking this class because it is required for my degree plan. I would eventually like to have a career in 2D animation - either working on full length feature films or on animated series. In addition to being a full time student, I work part time at my grandfather's machine shop and have a fairly successful (lately) ebay store re-selling mainly merch from popular animation series. I have had the opportunity to photograph several fundraising events for Autism Speaks Houston and hope to expand into doing more freelance photography work in the future.
